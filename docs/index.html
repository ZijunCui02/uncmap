About this map

Built to help choose an off-campus rental near UNC Chapel Hill. Target: 1b1b / studio, or a shared 2b2b — in-unit washer & dryer required, budget ≤ $2,000 / person.

The layers are derived from research, not guesses:

  • Destination — the UNC Biology teaching/lab building with the most upper-division class hours, from the BIOL curriculum and class schedules.
  • Amenities — groceries, dining streets, shopping, parks, landmarks.
  • Neighborhoods & listings — where UNC students cluster, by housing type.
  • Bus lines — Chapel Hill Transit routes drawn in their official colors.

This is step 1 of an ongoing build. Markers, filters, and an exhaustive listing sweep come next.
